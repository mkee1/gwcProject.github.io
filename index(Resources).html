How should I interact with people that have special needs?

People with special needs are people too! Like anyone, they have real feelings, so we should follow the appropriate terminology when talking about or talking to somenone with special needs. There are many false stereotypes and incorrect assumptions about people with special needs.

The best advice when interacting with someone with special needs is to get to know the person based on his or her personality rather than his or her disability!

Special Needs Organizations

Special Needs Organizations provide communities for individuals with special needs and their families. Never doubt the importance of a strong support system!

  • Parent to Parent USA

Parent to Parent USA offers parent to parent support as a core resource for families with children who have a special health card need, disability, or mental health concern.


  • Autism Speaks

Austism Speaks is advocates, creates awareness, and funds programs that provide people with autism with social and educational experiences.


  • Easter Seals

Easter Seals provides opportunities for people of all ages with a range of disabilities to achieve their full potential.
