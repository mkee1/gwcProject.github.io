Our Message:

There are roughly 6.7 million students receiving special needs education. Together, let’s provide resources and support for young minds in need of some help. All of us can be a friend!

We can’t cover all special needs topics (yet) but we do have plenty of resources on this website. Feel free to explore and share with others.
